Open positions

I am looking for a bright and motivated student to reserach and develop a new wearable technology for continuous and non-invasive biofluid analysis. Prospective PhD students can apply for a fully-funded PhD postion covering 3.5 years of full stipend and tuition fees (at UK rate). More details and online application available here . Feel free to email me directly if you would like to submit an application for this post.

If you are interested in joing the team as a PhD student in a topic different from the above, such as in microelectronics design, biosensors, and lab-on-CMOS, you are welcome get in touch via email, attaching your CV and transcripts. Please note that due to the large number of emails I receive, I may not respond to those with no CV/transcripts attached or where no refrenece is made to the financial needs/requirements of the candidate. You may find the list of scholarships available at UCL to find the one you might be eligible to apply for.

Any funded postdoctoral reserach positions will be announced here as soon as they become available. If you would like to apply for a postdoctoral fellowship to join the team, please email me directly and attach your CV, a brief desription of the topic you suggest and how it matches the current reserach in our team.
