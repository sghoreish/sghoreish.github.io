Open positions

I am always looking for strong PhD candidates in the field of microelectronics and biomedical engineering. Prospective students with background in these fields are welcome to contact me by email and include their CV and undergraduate transcript. Please note that I will announce any funded PhD posts/projects, if available, on this page. Otherwise, you may check your eligibility and consider applying for an appropriate UCL PhD scholarship.
