Open positions

I am always looking for high-calibre researchers to join my team for PhD and postdoctoral research on analogue IC design, electrochemical nano/biosensors, lab-on-CMOS and wearable biosensors.
If you are interested in joing my team for your PhD studies please get in touch via email, attaching both your CV and transcripts. Please note that due to the large number of emails I receive, I may not respond to those with no CV/transcripts attached or where no refrenece is made to the financial requirements of the candidate. You may find the list of scholarships available at UCL to find the one you might be eligible to apply for. Other funded PhD positions can be found on the UCL EPSRC DTP page

Any funded postdoctoral reserach positions will be announced here as soon as they become available. If you would like to apply for a postdoctoral "fellowship" (e.g. RAEng, Marie Curie, EPSRC postdoctoral fellowships) to join my team, please email me directly and attach your CV, a one-paragraph desription of your proposed reserach and briefly how it relates to current reserach in my team. Due to the large number of emails I receive, I may not respond to those with incomplete information
