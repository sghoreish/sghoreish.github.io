Outreach

I am pleased to take part in the launch of a new toy award called Science Toy Award . The award aims to celebrate and support outstanding toys that help develop STEM skills in children through play. This year, the toy-candidates were short-listed during Imperial Festival and the winner was selected by primary school students in the UK.
